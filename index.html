Welcome

'Band of Brothers' is a men's peer-to-peer club, set-up by local men - for local men; built around connection, social inclusion and emotional resilience, focusing on mental health, wellbeing and a shared commitment to positive change.

The 'Band of Brothers'
Pledge:

1. Reduce the high suicide rates in men;

2. Challenge the stigma around men expressing their emotions;

3. Work to reduce violence against women and girls within our Community.
