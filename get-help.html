Getting Help!

There are a number of supportive organizations that can help, including:

Samaritans

Samaritans works to make sure there's always someone there for anyone who needs someone.

Mind

We're Mind, the mental health charity. We're here to make sure no one has to face a mental health problem alone.

You can also find ongoing support through the following Organizations:

MANUP?

A registered mental health charity that breaks stigma by encouraging openness. Change people's thoughts, opinions & minds about men & mental health.

Men's Minds Matter

Men's Minds Matter are specialists in suicide intervention & prevention. Male suicide rates are unacceptably high but we will change this.

Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM)

We're the Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM), a suicide prevention charity on a mission to help people end their misery, not their lives.

Let’s Talk About Suicide Campaign

ANDYSMANCLUB are a men's suicide prevention charity, offering free-to-attend peer-to-peer support groups across the United Kingdom and online.

Tough to Talk

Tough to Talk partner with Organisations to meet the unique needs of their men, to break stigma and re-educate on the real issues that are pushing men beyond the brink.

Movember Foundation

The Movember Foundation provide reliable, expert information to help men cope and live happier, healthier, longer lives – no matter what life throws at us.
